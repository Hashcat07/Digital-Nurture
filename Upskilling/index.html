Community Bulletin

The city council has announced new plans for the upcoming summer festival. All residents are encouraged to participate and volunteer for various roles available across departments.

Road maintenance on Oak Street will continue through the end of the month. Alternate routes via Maple Avenue are recommended during peak hours to avoid delays.

The public library has extended its hours on weekends. New reading programs for children aged 6 to 12 are available every Saturday morning starting next week.

Residents can now apply for community garden plots online. Limited spots are available and will be allocated on a first-come, first-served basis.
